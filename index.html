📄 My Pages
👋 Welcome! Here's what you can do in this Notion-style editor:
  • 👉 Type '/' to open the block menu (heading, code, quote, todo)
  • 🔤 Press Enter to create a new block
  • 🔙 Press Backspace on empty block to delete it
  • 📦 Drag & Drop blocks to rearrange them
  • 📄 Create Multiple Pages from the left sidebar
  • 💾 Everything auto-saves in your browser
